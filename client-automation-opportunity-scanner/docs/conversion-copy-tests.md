# Conversion Copy Tests

## Final CTA Lead-In

Current production copy:

- Heading: "Show me where the work is getting stuck."
- Body: "I will help identify the first system worth cleaning up, whether that is follow-up, reporting, automation, or practical AI adoption."
- Proof points: "Clear first read", "No pressure pitch", "Practical next step"

## A/B Test Candidates

When traffic and analytics are ready, test the current heading against:

1. "Tell me what is slowing the business down."
2. "What is taking too much time, getting missed, or hard to measure?"

## Hypothesis

Small business owners should respond best to grounded language that names the operational drag directly before introducing automation, analytics, or AI as possible fixes.
